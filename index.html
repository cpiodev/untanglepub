Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 7, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Untangle, developed by CLNTgames, handles information in relation to the use of the application.

1. Overview

We do not collect or store personal data directly. However, the app uses third-party services that may automatically collect certain information.

2. Information Collected by Third Parties

The app integrates third-party SDKs such as Google AdMob and analytics tools. These services may collect information including:

  • Device information (device model, OS version)
  • App usage data (interactions, session duration)
  • Advertising identifiers (such as Google Advertising ID)
  • IP address (used for approximate location and fraud prevention)

3. Purpose of Data Collection

  • Providing and improving app functionality
  • Analyzing usage and performance
  • Displaying advertisements
  • Preventing fraud and abuse

4. Third-Party Services

The app relies on the following services:

  • Google AdMob (advertising)
  • Google Firebase Analytics (analytics)
  • Google Play Services (in-app purchases, potential game services)

These providers may process data according to their own privacy policies.

5. In-App Purchases

Purchases are processed securely via Google Play. We do not collect or store any payment information.

6. Data Storage and Retention

We do not store personal data on our own servers. Data collected by third-party services is retained according to their respective policies.

7. User Rights

Users may request access, correction, or deletion of their data directly through the relevant third-party providers (e.g., Google).

8. Children’s Privacy

The app is not directed to children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Security

We rely on trusted third-party providers that implement appropriate security measures.

10. International Data Transfers

Data collected by third-party services may be processed and stored in countries outside of your own, including the United States.

11. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.

12. Contact

If you have any questions regarding this policy, you can contact us at:
