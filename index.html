Privacy Policy

Last updated: 07/04/2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Untangle (published by CLNTgames) handles user data.

1. Data Collection

We do not collect or store personal data directly.

However, the app uses third-party services that may collect certain information automatically, including:

  • Device information (e.g. device model, OS version)
  • Usage data (e.g. app interactions, session duration)
  • Advertising identifiers

2. Purpose of Data Usage

The collected data is used for:

  • Analytics and app improvement
  • Displaying advertisements

3. Third-Party Services

The app uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob (advertising)
  • Analytics tools (such as Google Firebase Analytics)

These services may collect and process data according to their own privacy policies.

4. In-App Purchases

The app offers in-app purchases handled by Google Play. We do not process or store payment information.

5. Data Storage

We do not store user data on our own servers. Data collected by third-party services is managed according to their policies.

6. User Rights

Since we do not directly store personal data, requests for access or deletion should be directed to the relevant third-party providers.

7. Children’s Privacy

This app is not directed toward children under 13.

8. Security

We rely on trusted third-party services to ensure appropriate data protection.

9. Changes

This policy may be updated. Changes will be reflected on this page.
